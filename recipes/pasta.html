Spaghetti Carbonara

How to make this Spaghetti Carbonara Recipe

Ingredients

  • 240g Pasta
  • 100g guanciale
  • 2 eggs
  • 40g parmesan
  • 40g pecorino Romano
  • Olive Oil
  • Salt and pepper

Steps

  1. Dice the guanchiale and saute in olive oil until translucent.
  2. Cook the pasta in salted water until al dente.
  3. Whisk the eggs with the two types of cheese and add salt and pepper.
  4. Heat the guanchiale again,add cooking water until it boils.Add pasta and mix.
  5. Let the pasta sit before coating it with some more cheese.
